About

Mr. Brick Artist is Michael Nguyen, a multi-media artist of both traditional and digital practices. He drew pictures and played with Lego bricks since elementary school. Having survived the great 80's and nasty 90's, inspiration comes from popular culture of the eras as well as the mundanity of the everyday world, embracing the evolution of visual design as time presses on. Michael now partakes in this collective evolution by building modernized representations of cutting edge media with Lego bricks, while providing their instruction collections so that communities of builders can participate for a shared experience born from the medium's limitless creativity.

 

Mr. Brick Artist Community

Rebrickable:

For building enthusiasts and appreciators, the Rebrickable community builds and releases instructions for wonderful buildable models on a daily basis. This link will transport you to Mr.BrickArtist's shop page for instruction collections. You can manage your own collections and visit other builder's for constant inspiration.
Patreon:

For Lego enthusiasts and appreciators who really want to support Mr. Brick Artist's growth and production of continuous releases; Patreon is the high priority feed which brings early happenings of design processes such as in-progress shots, production commentary, and release data so that you are in touch with the creation process. Dedicated subscribers receive shout-outs for your patronage and positive interactions in upcoming videos and posts.
Youtube: Lego, informative content, and Mr.Brick Artist product line videos for all Rebrickable instruction releases.
